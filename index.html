About me

My name is Holly, and I'm an orchestral conductor around the world by day, and a web and software developer by night.I'm a maker, a solutions-strategist, and a champion for kindness.

I'm interested in the intersection of music and digital technology, and the possibilities for heightened creativity in real-world and networked spaces.

I'm passionate about smart solutions for a cleaner and fairer world, commissioning new creative works and skewing society towards seeing the arts as something to do rather than something to consume.
